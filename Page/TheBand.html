About us

Nigel Gatherer and Seonaid Lynn have also guested with the band, and we have been lucky to have the following people as callers: John & Ruby Wilkinson, John Watkins, Janet Johnston and Christine McIntosh. The band still includes some founder members, and current line-up is: fiddlers Anna McFadden, John Robertson and Claire Murdoch (also whistle), accordion Allan Gray, flute Donald Easton, drums Tom Bullimore, concertina Ted Tracey-Bower and keyboard Trish Santer.
