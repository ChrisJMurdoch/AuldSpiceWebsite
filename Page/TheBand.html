About us

Auld Spice have been playing together as a Ceilidh & Function Band since 1997. We regularly play in and around Edinburgh at weddings, parties, ceilidhs, singalongs, community events, senior citizen clubs, day centres, schools. With a comprehensive Ceilidh set list and able to provide a caller, we could be just what you are looking for when planning your next function. Come on in and find out a little bit more about the band. Feel free to give us a call or fill out our booking form if you would like to discuss how we might be able help you turn your function into a special event... The band originally formed from the Edinburgh Adult Learning Project in 1997.
