Auld Spice Ceilidh Band

Edinburgh ceilidh band the Auld Spice Band have been performing since 1997. We play regularly in and around Edinburgh at weddings, parties, ceilidhs, singalongs, community events, senior citizen clubs, day centres and schools, garden and park events, and local fairs.
